Your story, my focus.
Hi, I’m Marc Paraschiv — a passionate photographer based in Bucharest, Romania. Photography, for me, is more than just capturing images — it’s about freezing moments that carry emotion, personality, and beauty. Whether I’m out in nature or working one-on-one with someone, I’m always looking for that spark of realness that makes a photo feel alive.
I specialize in portrait and nature photography, offering personalized photo sessions that bring out the best in every subject. From intimate solo shoots to expressive outdoor scenes, my work is all about storytelling through light, composition, and emotion. I love working with natural environments and genuine expressions, creating images that feel both artistic and grounded. I offer services tailored to your needs — whether you're seeking creative portraits, scenic landscapes, or something in between. I focus on comfort, clarity, and creativity during every shoot, making sure you feel seen and represented.
Each project is a collaboration, and I’m always open to new ideas and inspiration. If you’re looking for a photographer who blends technical skill with a personal touch, I’d love to work with you and help turn your vision into something timeless.
